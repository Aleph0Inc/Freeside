  • Administration
    • View/Edit service definitions - Services are items you offer to your customers.
    • View/Edit package definitions - One or more services are grouped together into a package and given pricing information. Customers purchase packages, not services.
    • View/Edit agent types - Agent types define groups of package definitions that you can then assign to particular agents.
    • View/Edit agents - Agents are resellers of your service. Agents may be limited to a subset of your full offerings (via their type).
    • View/Edit referrals - Where a customer heard about your service. Tracked for informational purposes.
    • View/Edit locales and tax rates - Change tax rates, or break down a country into states, or a state into counties and assign different tax rates to each.
    • View/Edit POPs - Points of Presence
    • View/Edit invoice events - Actions for overdue invoices
